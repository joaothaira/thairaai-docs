ThairaAI includes native **Gmail** and **Google Calendar** views so you can read mail and check your schedule without leaving the app — and turn any email into an AI conversation with one click.

These views reuse the Google account you already connected in **Settings → Google**. No extra login is required.

<Aside type="note">
  The views are **native** — they call the Gmail and Calendar APIs directly and render the data with ThairaAI's own UI. They are not an embedded `web.whatsapp.com`-style browser, because Google blocks account sign-in inside embedded webviews ("this browser or app may not be secure").
</Aside>

## Prerequisites

Connect your Google account first — see [OAuth Setup](/en/google/oauth-setup). Once connected, the views work immediately using the existing tokens.

If the account is not connected, the page shows a prompt linking to **Settings → Google**.

## Opening the views

Click the **Google** entry at the top of the sidebar. The page opens with two tabs: **Gmail** and **Calendar**.

## Gmail

| Action | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Browse** | The most recent emails are listed with sender, subject, snippet, and date |
| **Read** | Click any email to open the full message in a side panel |
| **Load more** | A button at the bottom of the list fetches the next page of emails |

Email bodies are rendered inside a fully sandboxed frame, so remote scripts and tracking pixels in HTML emails cannot run.

### Analyze an email with AI

Each email has a **Google icon** button (also available inside the open-email panel). Clicking it:

1. Fetches the full email content
2. Opens a new chat with the email already pre-filled as the prompt
3. Lets you pick your assistant and send — the AI then summarizes, drafts a reply, extracts action items, or whatever you ask

<Aside type="tip">
  Chats started from an email are marked with the **Gmail logo** in the chat-history sidebar, so you can tell at a glance which conversations are about an email.
</Aside>

## Calendar

The Calendar tab offers three views:

| View | Shows |
|---|---|
| **Upcoming** | An agenda list of upcoming events grouped by day |
| **Week** | A seven-day grid; each column lists that day's events |
| **Month** | A classic month grid with event chips per day |

Use the **‹ ›** arrows to move between weeks/months, and **Today** to jump back to the current date. Today is always highlighted.

The Calendar uses the calendar configured in **Settings → Google** (the appointment calendar), falling back to your primary calendar when none is set.

## Privacy

All data is fetched on demand and rendered locally. Nothing is stored remotely — emails and events stay in your local session.
