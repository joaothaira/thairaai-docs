The first time you open ThairaAI, a full-screen guided onboarding walks you through everything you
need to get productive — your account, terms, appearance, email & calendar, and (optionally) WhatsApp.
It only appears once; you can revisit any of it later in **Settings**.

## The steps

<Steps>

1. **Sign up**

   Create your ThairaAI account. **Continue with Google** is the fastest path: a single Google
   consent signs you into the managed cloud **and** connects your Gmail and Google Calendar at the same
   time — you'll never be asked to authorize Google twice. You can also sign up with email and password.

2. **Terms & Privacy**

   Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before continuing.

3. **Appearance**

   Pick light or dark mode (onboarding starts in dark). You can change it anytime in Settings.

4. **Email & Agenda**

   Connect Gmail and Google Calendar so the assistant can read your email and manage your schedule. If
   you signed up with Google, this is already connected.

   <Aside type="tip">
   The assistant reads your Google Calendar to know when you're free, so just block your unavailable
   times (lunch, nights, days off) directly in the calendar — there's no separate "business hours" field
   to maintain.
   </Aside>

5. **WhatsApp**

   Connect WhatsApp so the assistant can answer on your behalf. This step is its own short wizard — see
   below. You can skip it and set it up later.

</Steps>

## WhatsApp: the two-number model

WhatsApp onboarding is a small guided wizard because it involves two different numbers:

- **Business number** — the WhatsApp line that runs ThairaAI. Your clients message it and an AI
  receptionist answers on your behalf.
- **Your personal number** — the line *you* use to chat with the assistant (read email, check your
  agenda, etc.) with full access.

The wizard runs in this order:

<Steps>

1. **How it works** — a quick explanation of the two-number model.

2. **Business profile** *(optional)* — your business name, professional name, and a welcome message.
   Completing this turns on **Business Mode** *before* your business WhatsApp goes live, so new contacts
   are greeted by the receptionist instead of receiving a setup code.

3. **Your personal number** — entered here and registered as the **owner** (full access). After your
   business WhatsApp links, a confirmation message is sent to this number.

4. **Connect (QR)** — scan the QR code with the phone that uses your **business** number. This is the
   last step, so by the time the line is live everything else is already configured.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
ThairaAI distinguishes two roles automatically. The **owner** (your personal number) talks to the full
assistant. Business contacts are auto-approved as **clients** and get the receptionist experience — they
never receive a pairing code. You can see and manage both under **Settings → WhatsApp → Authorized
Users**, where each contact shows an **Owner** or **Client** badge.
</Aside>
